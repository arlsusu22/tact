**Last Updated:** February 28, 2026

Need help with tact? Contact us and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

## Contact

- Email: [thetactapp@gmail.com](mailto:thetactapp@gmail.com)
- App Name: tact
- Platform: iOS

## Support Topics

We can help with:
- App setup and Screen Time authorization
- Subscription and billing questions
- Notification issues
- Bug reports and unexpected behavior
- Privacy and data handling questions

## Subscription Help

- Manage or cancel your subscription from iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
- Payments and refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store
- For refund requests, contact Apple Support directly

## Troubleshooting Basics

Before contacting support, please check:
- iOS version is 16.0 or newer
- Screen Time permissions are enabled
- Notifications are enabled for tact
- Low Power Mode and Focus settings are not blocking notifications

## What to Include in Your Email

To speed up support, include:
- Device model (for example, iPhone 15)
- iOS version
- tact app version
- Short description of the issue
- Screenshots if relevant

We do not ask for or need your personal Screen Time data.
